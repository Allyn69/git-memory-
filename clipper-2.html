Access anywhere

Access your clipped webpages on any computer, tablet, or phone - even when you're offline.

First make sure OneNote is installed.Then do the following:

  1. Open a browser, and tap the Share menu.

  2. Swipe left on the apps and tap More.

  3. Turn on OneNote and tap Done.

  4. From any webpage, tap the OneNote icon to save!
